ABOUT US

The Entrepreneurship Cell of NITK Surathkal aims to create, foster and promote the spirit of entrepreneurship by helping young and talented minds pursue the path of innovation and set up their own entrepreneurial ventures. We strongly believe in nurturing critical thinking and business acumen by conducting workshops, speaker sessions, innovative games and competitions as well as providing student entrepreneurs with access to resources such as seed funding, mentoring, consultancy and networking.

“E-Cell NITK aims to connect the creative minds with the right resources and to provide them with a platform to unlock their true potential.”
- Yash, Convenor

“At E-Cell NITK, we hope to provide abode to the students with a strong desire for business and entrepreneurship to hone their skills and successfully achieve their dreams.”
- Archita, President

“A vision is something you see and others don't. Don't worry about being successful but work towards being significant. Do what you love and you will never work a day in your life. Entrepreneurshipis about living your dreams.”
- Dr. Ritanjali Majhi, Faculty Advisor

Learning Portal

E-Cell NITK believes that constant learning is key to entrepreneurial success. To help kickstart this process, we present our own specially made courses that focus on all the essential skills required to start a business. These courses are designed to orient students towards entrepreneurship and strengthen their business acumen and decision making while also catering to the interests of all the business enthusiasts out there.

STARTUP INTERNSHIP
PROGRAM

Startup Internship Program is a nascent initiative of E-Cell NITK. Through SIP, we aim to provide exposure to start-up culture by collaborating with start-ups willing to offer internship opportunities to the students of NITK Surathkal thereby providing a platform for highly driven individuals to get relevant experience in their desired fields and hopefully, start their own entrepreneurial journey.

Enterprise

Enterprise is one of our primary events and is designed to provide freshers with first-hand experience in entrepreneurship. The event encompasses everything from building your own B-Plan to generating revenue and gives the participants greater insight into the crucial role teamwork plays in the success of any business venture.

EVENTS

E-Summit

The annual Entrepreneurship Summit is the flagship event of E-Cell NITK. Essentially a celebration of the entrepreneurial spirit, it is a multi-faceted event that encompasses talks by eminent entrepreneurs, business workshops, a case study competition, a business quiz and a pitching event where startups are given the chance to interact and connect with investors and mentors.

Hult Prize

The Hult prize has been named The “Nobel Prize for students” from media sources worldwide. With a $1million global startup prize as it’s anchor activity, the Hult Prize has brought impact focused programs, events and trainings to over a million students globally, creating a pathway for youths everywhere to take action to build a better world.
